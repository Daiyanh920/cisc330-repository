Ami sesh

This business will be a all you can eat buffet. This buffet will provide you the finest Asian dishes from different parts of Asia. We will have Chinese, Korean, Japanese, Vietnam, Indian, Nepal, Indonesian, etc. The meaning behind my Business name is a Bengali phrase, which translates to "I'm done" which people say when they are done eating.

Food types

We are going serve the most popular dishes from different parts of Asia. Were going to have hot food, cold food, snacks, desserts, and drinks. We will serve different dishes depending on the season. Our foods won't be the same when you vist. For example we will have Tteokbokki, Hotteok, and Manduguk. These are Korean dishes ate during the winter times, and not only will we have winter foods we will also have foods for the spring, summer, and fall. You won't be eating the same thing when you vist.
